Title: Searching for all ebuilds with specific useflag in Gentoo Tags: - configuration - eix - gentoo - linux - terminal - useflag meta: _edit_last: '486411' Ever wondered how to find all those programms in portage you didn't install, which have a specific useflag you were interested in ? Well, eix is capable of this useful feature, just search like this: eix -U someuseflag
